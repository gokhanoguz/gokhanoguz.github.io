  • Payment is charged to your Apple ID or Google account at confirmation of purchase.
  • The subscription automatically renews unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.
  • You can manage and cancel auto-renewal at any time from your Apple ID account settings (iOS) or Google Play subscriptions (Android).
  • The current price for each plan is shown in-app before purchase. Pricing may vary by region and is subject to change.
  • Refund requests must be directed to the store where the purchase was made (Apple or Google), in line with their respective refund policies.

The free tier of Sesla is available indefinitely without charge and includes the home board, the Food and Feelings categories, one user profile, and the on-device text-to-speech voices provided by your device.
