Sesla is a one-developer project. Bug reports, feature requests, and accessibility feedback are read personally and matter a lot.

Get in touch

Email: m.gokhanoguz2@gmail.com

For the fastest answer, please include:

  • Your device model and OS version (iOS or Android).
  • Sesla version (Settings → About).
  • What you tried to do, what happened, and what you expected.
  • A screenshot if it's a visual issue.

Subscription help

Sesla Plus subscriptions are billed and managed by Apple or Google. To change or cancel:

  • iOS: Settings app → tap your name → Subscriptions → Sesla.
  • Android: Play Store app → profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → Sesla.

Refunds are handled by the store you bought from. Apple's process is at reportaproblem.apple.com; Google's is at play.google.com/store/account/orderhistory.

Restoring purchases

If you reinstall Sesla or move to a new device, sign in to the same Apple ID or Google account that bought the subscription, then open the paywall and tap Restore purchases.

Reporting accessibility issues

Sesla is built for non-speaking users and their families. If something blocks you from communicating, please flag it as "accessibility" in the email subject line. These reports jump the queue.
