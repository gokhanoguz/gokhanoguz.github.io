Deckstiny

A roguelike deckbuilder with elemental Resonance & Fusion.

In development

Ascend the Shattered Spire one floor at a time. Build a deck from scratch each run, weave four elements into resonant chains, and switch elements at the perfect moment to trigger devastating fusion combos. Every run is different. Every choice matters. Die and start over with new knowledge.

What makes it different

Resonance

Play same-element cards in sequence to build stacking power — up to +100% damage and block. Spend it on an empowered play when it matters most.

Fusion

Switch elements at the right moment to trigger combo effects — Steam Blast, Void Strike, Storm Front, and three more. Six fusions, six identities.

Element Channeling

Cards that let you sculpt your hand. Elemental strategy is deliberate, not luck-of-the-draw.

Run variety

Three heroes, branching maps, narrative events, an ascension ladder, and a daily challenge with a globally seeded run.

The four elements

🔥 Fire ❄️ Ice ⚡ Lightning 🌑 Shadow

Each element has its own combat identity — Fire piles on burn and pressure, Ice freezes and walls up, Lightning chains and surges, Shadow weakens and exhausts. Heroes have a primary element (+30% damage) and a secondary element (+15%), so the same card plays differently in different hands.
