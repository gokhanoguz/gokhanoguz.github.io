What it is

Forgotten Script is a single-player cipher-decoding puzzle in the tradition of newspaper cryptograms. Each run hands you a series of substitution-cipher inscriptions of escalating difficulty, ending in a famous-quote finale. Your job is to figure out the alphabet that was used to write them.

The puzzles are deterministic from a daily seed, so your daily puzzle is the same one every other player gets that day — but you can also play unlimited endless runs whenever you like.

Features

Daily & endless runs

One curated daily puzzle for everyone, plus unlimited endless runs at four difficulty tiers.

Three languages

Play and decode in English, Turkish, or Spanish — full UI localisation and language-native quote banks.

Streaks & leaderboards

Optional sign-in unlocks daily streaks, all-time and weekly leaderboards, and cross-device progress sync.

Original inscriptions

Hand-written, never-before-seen aphorisms mixed in with the famous quotes — no recognition shortcuts.

Calm by default

No ads, no notifications begging you back, no dark patterns. Sit with one puzzle for as long as you like.

Plays offline

The whole puzzle engine runs locally. Sign-in and leaderboards are optional; sync resumes when you're back online.
