About

LandRush is a real-world territory game where your movement draws the map. Walk, run, or bike through your neighborhood — close a loop and the land inside becomes yours. Compete with friends, join clans, challenge rivals, earn Rush Coins, and customize your presence on the map. Every step you take expands your empire.

Key Features

  • GPS Territory Claiming — Walk, run, or bike to draw loops on the map. The enclosed area becomes your territory.
  • PvP Territory Wars — Other players can walk through your land and steal it. Defend with shields or reclaim it.
  • 1v1 Challenges — Challenge friends to distance, area, or activity competitions with coin wagers.
  • Clans — Create or join a clan. Pool territory, donate to the treasury, unlock rare sigils, and compete in clan wars.
  • Daily Missions & Streaks — Complete missions for coin rewards. Maintain your streak for up to 2× earning multiplier.
  • Events — Community-wide competitions with time-limited leaderboards and exclusive rewards.
  • Cosmetics Shop — Avatar frames, territory patterns, banners, and border effects.
  • Companion Mode — Invite friends to join your walk. Territory splits equally.
