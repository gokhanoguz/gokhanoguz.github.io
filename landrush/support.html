Before you write

Most issues clear up after a quick check:

  • Make sure you're on the latest version from the Play Store or App Store.
  • Restart the app (force-quit, then re-open).
  • Check that Location permissions are set to "Always" or "While Using" — LandRush needs GPS to track your movement.
  • If territory isn't being captured, make sure you're closing a complete loop and walking at a normal pace.

What to include in a bug report

  • Your device model and OS version (e.g. "Samsung S24, Android 16").
  • LandRush version (shown at the bottom of the Profile page).
  • A short description of what happened and what you expected.
  • A screenshot or screen recording, if possible.
